specifications:
    inputs: Length in feet and inches
    outuput: Equivalent length in centimeters

knowns:
    1 inch = 2.54 centimeters
    1 foot = 12 inches

algorithm
    1. Get the length in feet and inches
    2. Convert length into total inches
    3. Convert total inches into centimeters
    4. Output centimeters

variable list:
    int feet -> store given feet
    int inches -> store given inches
    int totalInches -> store total inches
    int centimeters -> store length in centimeters
